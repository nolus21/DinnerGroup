    Process


    The dinner.group is not so much a 'company' in a traditional sense, but a method of making decisions and generating ideas that lead to meaningful results.

    In the introductory stage of any significant project, a kind of salon is created where the project lives or dies over the course of an evening.

    Invited group members, specialist consultants, academics, clients and friends meet over dinner for a round table discussion and research a project proposal
    Hierarchy is dissolved and the topic is both explored and critiqued until a consensus is made.

    Those who are inspired to continue with the project do so, allowing the ideas discussed to generate the intellectual energy required to carry a project through.

    Operating in this way ​allows worthy projects to thrive and creative ideas to flourish.
    Dinner participants may 'opt in' to a project according to their expertise or interest, which leads to greater flexibility, effectiveness and energy invested into each project.
